Second Part

Our User

  • Someone who cares about always being in time
  • Someone who doesn´t like how their alarm wakes them up
  • Someone who has had problems waking up
  • Anyone with the desire of improving a part,even if it is little, of their lifestyle

Proposed Solution

Our proposed solution is an app that has alarm levels and also checks when you are putting the alarm, that you normally put in the morning(AM), in the afternoon(PM). The levels of the alarm will determine the complexity of the "Snooze" or "Dismiss" of the alarm. Also we want to put "More configurations" which will allow the user to wake up of different ways, like ligthing up the flashligth or gradually increasing the volumne of the alarm. This with the objective that the alarm isn´t a schock in your body allowing you to wake up more pleasantly

Main Tasks

  • Lock your phone and be confident about your alarme waking you up
